Dear Parent / Teacher / Children,

WHAT WOULD YOU LIKE YOUR CHILD TO ACHIEVE?


Every child has a different learning curve. Some can grasp a topic within no time while others require regular practice. It is the parents and teachers who best understand the requirements and the pace of your child/ren.

With that in mind, we have added our newest facility, HC Goals, which provides a powerful tool to parents and teachers to set the learning objectives for your child/ren.

HC Goals:

  • Lets you set goals for each of your child/ren separately,

  • Lets you set targets by Level / Topic / Subtopic,

  • Lets you and your child keep track of his progress in every individual topic,

  • And, much more.

Sign in to your HC account to set the first goal for your child now. Also, do send us your input about how we can improve this feature. We want to hear from you.

OTHER UPDATES

We have finished adding detailed solutions for every type of problem for our 5th and 6th graders. So, no worries if you got a question wrong.. Our explanations will surely help you get it right the next time. Go ahead and practice and do not be afraid to make mistakes!
