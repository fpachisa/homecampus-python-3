MORE FEATURES

Pretty soon what you will see on HC is the facility to design and create tests for your child. Tests will help you find out which areas s/he is excelling in and in which others there is a scope for improvement. Stay tuned for more information.


A TEASER FOR YOU, SIXTH GRADERS


Tara and Clara, who were standing 29.25 km apart, started running towards each other at 09 15. Tara ran at an average speed of 5.5 km/h while Clara ran at an average speed of 7.5 km/h. At what time did they meet?

If you found this problem easy, then that's fantastic!! Find more challenging Q&As at HC Practice.

If you found this problem a bit difficult, then worry not. Log in to your HC Practice for detailed solutions to all questions you attempt.

What's more? We've added several advanced problem sums recently on the topics of Fractions, Speed and Percentage especially for our Primary 6 children. See if you can crack them.


A QUESTION FOR YOU, FOURTH GRADERS

Is the shape on the right symmetric along the dotted line? If yes, how? If no, why not? Not sure, go to HC Learn to find out the answer.

Whether you want to brush up your knowledge or gain new knowledge, you are sure to find HC Notes handy. Other new topics added to HC Learn this past month include: Geometry: Rectangles and Squares; Symmetric figures, shapes and patterns; Measurement of Area and Perimeter of Rectangles and Squares; and Measurement of Time in Seconds, 24-hour Clock and Duration. Go, check them out!
