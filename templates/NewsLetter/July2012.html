ANSWER THIS, FOURTH GRADERS

Ken and Ben had 132 paper clips altogether. Ken gave away 17 paper clips to Ben. In the end, Ken had 3 times as many paper clips as Ben. How many paper clips did Ben have in the beginning?

YES, it's a problem that a fourth grader can solve and NO, she doesn't need to know algebra for that! Go to HC Learn to find out how to crack this and other word problems using the simple yet powerful method of models.

What else? Well, we've added lots more content for our fourth graders this last June. Check out some quick notes and short videos on Whole Numbers at HC Learn. Then, go do some practice or take a test to see if you understood what you just learnt.
