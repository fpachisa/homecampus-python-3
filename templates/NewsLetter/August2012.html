Trust all is well with you as it is with us! Here's what we've been up to the past month. Read on...


WE'VE READIED WHAT WE'D PROMISED

The tremendous success of HC Tests for Grade 4 has led us to work harder and faster to deliver HC Tests for grades 5 and 6.

Topical quizzes are available on Percentage, Ratio, Algebra, Geometry, Speed and Volume, among others. So, go ahead and take a few to check your knowledge!



MILESTONE REACHED

With the addition of the last few notes on Decimals and Fractions, the HC learning library has all it takes for our fourth graders to master maths at their level.

Some of the recent pages added to the website are Understanding Tenths, Hundredths and Thousandths, Adding Decimals, Subtracting Decimals, Multiplying Decimals, Dividing Decimals, Word Problems on Decimals, Fraction of a Set and Problem Sums on Fractions. Go, check them out!


NEXT THINGS

Fourth graders, we hope you have been practicing the basic questions at HC. Now, get ready to solve some problem sums! Expect a whole new array of word problems on several topics including Whole Numbers, Fractions, Decimals, Time, Money, Area & Perimeter, Geometry, and Tables & Line Graphs.

As requested by parents of our younger kids, the next major task we are undertaking at HC is to develop study material for lower levels of elementary starting with Grade 3. Tune in to for updates.



So long for now. Keep your comments, compliments, feedback and input coming. They keep us going!!

Have an awesome August!
