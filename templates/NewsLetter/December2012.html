CAN YOUR 6TH GRADER SOLVE THIS YET?

A rectangular juice dispenser has a base area of 450 cm2 and a height of 25 cm. Gale adds syrup and water in the ratio 1 : 3 to the dispenser to make a beverage. If he uses 6.75 litres of water, what percentage of the dispenser will be filled when the beverage is made?


No worries if s/he cannot crack it yet. The latest playlist of video lessons that we have added on Volume of Cubes and Cuboids will surely give her/him a jump start on solving such advanced problems.

Another playlist of videos we have recently added elaborately explains the concept of Pie Charts with illustrative examples.

Do go check out those videos then go practice similar problems or take a couple of HC Tests to check your understanding.
