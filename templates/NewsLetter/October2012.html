MADE PROGRESS ON HC PROGRESS!



If the questions above are what you have got, then it's the answers that we have got... with our reports. Go, check them out at HC Progress for a detailed insight into your child's performance.

Until then, if there is anything specific that you would like to see on HC Progress, give us a shout!

SOME QUICK UPDATES

With the addition of unlimited practice exercises on Fractions, we have finished putting together everything that our 4th graders need to learn, practice and progress in maths.

When you have learnt and practised these skills, quiz yourself with a few HC Tests.

By the way, we hope that our older learners, the 5th and 6th graders, are also taking advantage of the complete and comprehensive set of learning, practice and test tools we have at their disposal.
