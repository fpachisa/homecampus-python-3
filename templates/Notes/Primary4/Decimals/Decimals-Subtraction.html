4. Serena had $20 at first.
How much money had she left after spending $12.34 at a stationery shop?

Sum of money at first   =   $20.00
Sum of money spent   =   $12.34
$20  =  $20.00
Sum of money left   =   $20.00  −  $12.34
=   $7.66

She had $7.66 left.
