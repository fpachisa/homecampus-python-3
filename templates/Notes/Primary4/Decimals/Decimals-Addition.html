4. Serena spent $11.49 on a diary and 85 ¢ on a pen.
How much money did she spend altogether?

Cost of diary   =   $11.49
Cost of pen   =   $0.85
85 ¢  =  $0.85
Total cost   =   $11.49  +  $0.85
=   $12.34

She spent $12.34 altogether.
