How to Convert Hours to Minutes?

Hour Converter

1. How many minutes are there in 5 h 1 min?

We know,
1 h  =  60 min

So,
    5 h 
=  5 × 60 min
=  300 min

Therefore,
    5 h + 1 min 
=  300 min + 1 min
=  301 min

There are 301 minutes in 5 h 1 min.
