What is a number pattern?

Download Free Worksheets
Number pattern is a series of numbers that follows a particular pattern or sequence. There is a common connection between all the numbers. For example, the pattern between 0, 3, 6, 9, 12 is that the next number is derived by adding 3 to the previous number or in other words we are skip counting by 3 to get the next number.

What are different types of number patterns?

There are many types of numbers patterns such as:

  • Arithmetic patterns
  • Geometric patterns
  • Harmonic patterns
  • Fibonacci patterns
However, for our primary level mathematics we will mostly focus on arithmetic patterns where the number sequence is generated by either subtraction or addition of numbers.
