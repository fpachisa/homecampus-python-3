2. Find the place and value of each digit in the number 3267.



In 3267,
  • the digit 7 is in the ones place, its value is 7 × 1 or 7
  • the digit 6 is in the tens place, its value is 6 × 10 or 60
  • the digit 2 is in the hundreds place, its value is 2 × 100 or 200
  • the digit 3 is in the thousands place, its value is 3 × 1000 or 3000
