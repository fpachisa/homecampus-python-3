What are equivalent fractions?

Equivalent fractions are fractions that represents the same part of a whole. They have different numerators and denominators but with equal fractional values.

How to generate Equivalent Fractions?

Method 1: Multiply the numerator and the denominator by the same number.

Method 2: Divide the numerator and the denominator by the same number.

As long as you multiply or divide both numerator and denominator by the same number, the fractional value will not change.
