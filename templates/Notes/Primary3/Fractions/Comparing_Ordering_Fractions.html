Comparing and Ordering Fractions

Practice Unlimited Questions

How to compare fractions?

Step 1: Express the fractions using a common denominator. For this you need to find out lowest common denominator.
Step 2: Create equivalent fractions by making the denominator common.
Step 3: The fraction with the smallest numerator is the smallest fraction while the fraction with the biggest numerator is the biggest fraction.

For better understanding follow the example below.
