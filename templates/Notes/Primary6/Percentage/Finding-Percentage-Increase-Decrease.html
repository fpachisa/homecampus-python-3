Word Problem 5: Sue bought a piece of cloth 140 cm long. After washing it, the cloth shrank by 4% along its length. What is the length of the cloth now?

100%  −  4%  =  96%

96% of 140 cm  =  {{frGen(96,100)}}  ×  140 cm

= 134.4 cm

The length of the cloth is 134.4 cm now.
