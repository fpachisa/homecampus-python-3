How to Convert Hours to Microseconds?

Convert Hours to Microseconds:

Number of hours = 



1 hour  =  3,600,000,000 or 3.6 × 109 microseconds.

To convert hours to microseconds simply multiply the hours by 3600000000 to get microseconds.

1 hour  =  60 minutes

1 minute  =  60 seconds

1 second  =  1000 milliseconds

1 millisecond  =  1000 microseconds

So, 1 hour  =  60  ×  60  ×  1000  ×  1000  =  3600000000 microseconds

Example 1: convert 2 hours to microseconds.
Multiply 2 by 3600000000

Total microseconds in 2 hours  =  2  ×  3600000000  =  7.2 × 109

Hence, there are 7.2 × 109 microseconds in 2 hours.
Example 2: convert 0.00000008 hours to microseconds
Multiply 0.00000008 by 3600000000

Total microseconds in 0.00000008 hours  =  0.00000008  ×  3600000000  =  288

Hence, there are 288 microseconds in 0.00000008 hours.
