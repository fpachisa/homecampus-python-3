1 hour  =  60 minutes.

To convert hours to minutes simply multiply the hours by 60 to get minutes.

Example 1: convert 5 hours to minutes.
Multiply 5 by 60

Total minutes in 5 hours  =  5  ×  60  =  300

Hence, there are 300 minutes in 5 hours.
Example 2: convert 0.004 hours to minutes
Multiply 0.004 by 60

Total minutes in 0.004 hours  =  0.004  ×  60  =  0.24

Hence, there are 0.24 minutes in 0.004 hours.
