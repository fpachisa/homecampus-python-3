How to Convert Hours to Weeks?

1 week  =  7 days  =  168 hours

1 hour  =  1  ÷  168  =  0.00595 week

To convert hours to weeks simply divide the hours by 168 to get weeks.
Example 1: convert 700 hours to weeks.
Divide 700 by 168

Total weeks in 700 hours  =  700  ÷  168  =  4.167

Hence, there are 4.167 weeks in 700 hours.
Example 2: convert 24 hours to weeks
Divide 24 by 168

Total weeeks in 24 hours  =  24  ÷  168  =  0.14286

Hence, there is 0.14286 week in 24 hours.
