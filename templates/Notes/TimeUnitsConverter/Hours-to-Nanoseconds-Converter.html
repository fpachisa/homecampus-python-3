How to Convert Hours to Nanoseconds?

Convert Hours to Nanoseconds:

Number of hours = 



1 hour  =  3,600,000,000,000 or 3.6 × 1012 nanoseconds.

To convert hours to nanoseconds simply multiply the hours by 3600000000000 to get nanoseconds.

1 hour  =  60 minutes

1 minute  =  60 seconds

1 second  =  1000 milliseconds

1 millisecond  =  1000 microseconds

1 microsecond  =  1000 nanoseconds

So, 1 hour  =  60  ×  60  ×  1000  ×  1000  ×  1000  =  3600000000000 nanoseconds

Example 1: convert 2 hours to nanoseconds.
Multiply 2 by 3600000000000

Total nanoseconds in 2 hours  =  2  ×  3600000000000  =  7.2 × 1012

Hence, there are 7.2 × 1012 nanoseconds in 2 hours.
Example 2: convert 0.00000000008 hours to nanoseconds
Multiply 0.00000000008 by 3600000000

Total nanoseconds in 0.00000000008 hours  =  0.00000000008  ×  3600000000000  =  288

Hence, there are 288 nanoseconds in 0.00000000008 hours.
