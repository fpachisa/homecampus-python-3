How to Convert Meters to Miles?

Meters to Miles Converter:

Meters = 



1 meter  =  0.0006214 mile

1 mile  =  1609.34 meters
Example 1: convert 5000 meters to miles.
Divide 5000 by 1609.34

Miles in 5000 meters  =  5000  ÷  1609.34  =  3.107

Hence, there are 3.107 miles in 5000 meters.
Example 2: Distance from my home to school is 7540 m. How much is that in miles?
Divide 7540 meters by 1609.34

Miles in 7540 meters  =  7540  ÷  1609.34  =  4.685 miles

Hence, distance from my home to school is 4.685 miles.
