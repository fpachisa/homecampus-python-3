Example 1: convert 100 metres to kilometres.
Divide 100 by 1000

Kilometre in 100 meters  =  100  ÷  1000  =  0.1

Hence, there are 0.1 kilometres in 100 metres.
Example 2: Speed of light in m/s is 3 x 108. Convert it in km/s.
To conver metres in kilometres, simple divide it by 1000.

Speed of light in km/s  =  3 x 108  ÷  1000  =  3 x 105

Hence, speed of light is 3 x 108 m/s or 3 x 105 km/s
