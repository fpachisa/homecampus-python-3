How to Convert Meters to Centimeters?

Meters to Centimeters Converter:

Meters = 



1 meter  =  100 centimeters

1 centimeter  =  0.01 meters
Example 1: convert 100 meters to centimeters.
Multiple 100 by 100

Centimeters in 100 meters  =  100  ×  100  =  10000

Hence, there are 10000 centimeters in 100 meters.
Example 2: A basketball player is 2.35 meters tall. What is his height in centimeters?
Multiply 2.35 by 100

Height in centimeters  =  2.35  ×  100  =  235

Hence, height of the basketball player is 235 centimeters.
