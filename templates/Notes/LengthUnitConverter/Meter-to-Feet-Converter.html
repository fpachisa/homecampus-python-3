How to Convert Meters to Feet?

Meters to Feet Converter:

Meters = 



1 meter  =  3.28084 feet

1 foot  =  0.3048 meters
Example 1: convert 10 meters to feet.
Multiple 10 by 3.28084

Feet in 10 meters  =  10  ×  3.28084  =  32.8084

Hence, there are 32.8084 feet in 10 meters.
Example 2: my height in meters is 2.1. How much is it feet?
Multiple 2.1 by 3.28084

Feet in 2.1 meters  =  2.1  ×  3.28084  =  6.89 feet

Hence, I'm 6.89 feet or 6 feet 10 inches tall
