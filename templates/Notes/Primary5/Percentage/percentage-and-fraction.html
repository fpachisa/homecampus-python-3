2.  Following is a cookie box containing 100 assorted cookies of 4 different shapes.
a)  Find out the percentage of cookies that are heart-shaped.
b)  Find out the percentage of cookies that are round.
c)  Find out the percentage of cookies that are square-shaped.
d)  Find out the percentage of cookies that are star-shaped.

Heart-shaped cookies
=  {{ frGen(1,10) }}     (using Method 1)
=  {{ frGen(1,10) }}  ×  {{ frGen(10,10) }} (change denominator to 100)
=  {{ frGen(10,100) }}
=  10%
10 out of 100 cookies in the cookie box are heart-shaped.
  Or,
10% of the cookies are heart-shaped.
Round cookies
=  {{ frGen(1,4) }}     (using Method 1)
=  {{ frGen(1,4) }}  ×  {{ frGen(25,25) }} (change denominator to 100)
=  {{ frGen(25,100) }}
=  25%
25 out of 100 cookies in the cookie box are round.
  Or,
25% of the cookies are round.
Square-shaped cookies
=  {{ frGen(3,10) }} of 1 whole box     (using Method 2)
=  {{ frGenDel1(3,10,1) }}  ×  10010%     (1 whole = 100%)
=  30%
30% of the cookies are square-shaped.
Star-shaped cookies
=  {{ frGen(7,20) }} of 1 whole box     (using Method 3)
=  7  ÷  20 of 1 whole box
=  0.35 of 1 whole box
=  0.35  ×  100%     (1 whole = 100%)
=  35%
35% of the cookies are star-shaped.
