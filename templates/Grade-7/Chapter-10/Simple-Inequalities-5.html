8 > 6
Divide both sides by −4.
LHS: 8 × −4 = −2
RHS: 6 × −4 = −1.5
−2 < −1.5, as −2 appears to the left of −1.5 on the number line.
So, the inequality sign gets reversed.
As a rule...
  • When both sides of an inequality are multiplied or divided by a positive number, the inequality sign remains the same.

  • When both sides of an inequality are multiplied or divided by a negative number, the inequality sign gets reversed.
