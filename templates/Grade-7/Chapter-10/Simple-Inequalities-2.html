Example 1:
Represent the following relationships using inequalities:

a)
The storage temperature for packaged food products must not exceed 30°C.
b)
The normal fasting blood sugar level is between
70 mg/dl and 99 mg/dl, including both these values.
c)
The fine for skipping a traffic signal is at least $100.
d)
More than 50% of the population in the city is suffering from conjunctivitis.
Solution 1:
a)
Let t°C be the storage temperature for packaged food products.
t°C is less than or equal to 30°C.
So, t ≤ 30.
b)
Let x mg/dl be the normal fasting blood sugar level.
x mg/dl is more than or equal to 70 mg/dl and less than or equal to 99 mg/dl.
So, 70 ≤ x ≤ 99.
c)
Let $y be the fine for skipping a traffic signal.
$y is more than or equal to $100.
So, y ≥ 100.
