Example 8:
Nita appeared for a mathematics test containing 3 sections. She scored 57 marks in the first section and 28 marks in the second section. At least how many marks did she need in the last section to pass the test, given that the minimum passing marks in the test were 150?

Solution 8:
Let x be the marks needed in the last section.

Minimum passing marks = 150

The inequality:

x + 57 + 28
≥ 150

x + 85
≥ 150

x + 85 − 85
≥ 150 − 85

x
≥ 65

She needed at least 65 marks to pass the test.
