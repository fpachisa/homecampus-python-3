In x hours, the total distance travelled by the two cars   =   150 − 50   =   100 km
Distance = Speed × Time
Distance covered by car A = 60x km
Distance covered by car B = 50x km
60x + 50x = 100
110x = 100
So, x
  =   100 ÷ 110

  =   0.9 hours

  =   0.9 × 60

  =   54 minutes
