Example 4:
A man drove 100 km at an average speed of 40 km/h. He drove back the same distance at an average speed of 60 km/h.

a)
Find the average of the speeds 40km/h and 60 km/h.

b)
Find the average speed of the car for the whole journey.
Solution 4a)
Average of the speeds
  =   (40 + 60) ÷ 2

  =   50 km/h

b) 
Total distance covered   =   200 km
Time taken for one way journey
= 100 ÷ 40

= 2.5 h
