Example 21:
Charlie had a mass of 78 kg before joining a weight loss program. He lost 21% as a result of the program. Find his new mass.

Solution 21:
New value
= Original value × (100% − Percent decrease)
∴ New mass
=  
78 × (100 − 21)%

=  
78 × 79%

=  
61.62 kg
Example 22:
Rainfall recorded this year was 30% below average. If it rained 175 mm this year, find the average rainfall.

Solution 22:
175 mm = Average rainfall × (100 − 30)%
Average rainfall
=  
175 ÷ 0.7

=  
250 mm
