Example 18:
Saima's weak performance in the pre-final examination led her to a coaching institute. Her result in the final examination was 40% better with a score of 420. What was her score in the pre-final examination?

Solution 18:
Let x be Saima's score in the pre-final examination.
Then, 40%
=

(420 - x)
______

x

× 100%
So, x   =   300
Alternatively,
New value
= Original value × (100% + Percent increase)
So, Original value
=

New Value
________________

(100% + % increase)

Here,
Original value = Pre-final value
=

420
____________

(100% + 40%)

= 300
∴ Saima's score in the pre-final examination was 300.
