5.2 Reverse Percentages
Example 13:
Amar has been saving for a cricket bat that costs $80. The cricket bat costs 125% of the amount he has saved so far. How much money has Amar saved so far?

Solution 13:
Let $z be the amount of Amar's savings. Then, 125% of z is 80.
125% × z
  =  
80

z
  =  
80 ÷ 125 %

z
  =  
80 ÷ 1.25

  =  
64

∴ Amar has saved $64 so far.
