5.1.2 One Quantity as a Percentage of Another
A percent is a fraction that compares a part to a whole. In other words, percentage helps us understand how the two quantities are proportionally divided.
Do note
Percentage value of 100% corresponds to the number '1' which means that the two quantities being compared are equal.
Example 6:
In a box containing 10 mangoes, 7 were ripe and the rest were unripe. Find the percentage of unripe mangoes in the box.

Solution 6:
Number of unripe mangoes = 10 − 7 = 3
