Example 14:
A certain university has three grades of teaching staff - Assistant Professor, Professor and Head of Department (HOD). An HOD gets paid 100% more than an assistant professor. A professor gets 25% less salary than an HOD. If the sum of the monthly salaries of an assistance professor, a professor and an HOD is $45000, find the monthly salary of a professor.

Solution 14:

Then, the monthly salary of an HOD is
x + 100%x = 2x

= 1.5x
