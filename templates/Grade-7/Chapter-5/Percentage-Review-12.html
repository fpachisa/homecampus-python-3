Example 12:
Barbara scored a total of 560 marks in 6 subjects of 100 marks each in the year-end exam. Her friend Nancy scored 525 marks.
a) What is the percentage score of each girl?
b) Compare their scores as a percentage of one another.

Solution 12:
Maximum possible score = 600
a)
Barbara's score
=

560
____

600

× 100 %

= 93.33 %

Nancy's score
=

525
____

600

× 100 %

= 87.50 %

b)
Barbara's marks as a % of Nancy's marks
=

560
____

525

× 100 %

= 106.67 %
This means that Barbara scored 6.67% (106.67% − 100%) marks more than Nancy.
