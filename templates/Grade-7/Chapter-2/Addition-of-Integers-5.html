Example 12:
Evaluate 2 + (−3).
Solution 12:
Method 1:
2 + (−3)  =  +2 + (−3)
Start at +2 on the number line and move −3 units to the left. The answer is −1.
Method 2:
Subtract 2 from 3 (3 − 2 = 1) and put the sign of 3 in front (−). The answer is −1.
