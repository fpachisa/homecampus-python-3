Example 21:
Solve the following:
a) 56 ÷ [−(64)½]
b) 0 ÷ −8
c) (−55) ÷ 0
Solution 21:
a)
56 ÷ [−(64)½]
=  56 ÷ (−8)

=  −7

b)
0 ÷ −8 = 0
0 divided by any number is equal to 0.
c)
(−55) ÷ 0
A number divided by 0 is not defined.
For your understanding...
Division by 0 is not defined. Dividing is splitting into equal parts. Dividing a number by zero does not make sense!
