2.2.1 Adding two integers with the same sign
Example 8:
A boy is 2 years old. What will be his age 3 years later?
Solution 8:
Age of the boy after 3 years = 2 + 3
A number line can be used to show how two positive integers are added:
Start at positive 2 on the number line and move +3 units to the right. The answer is +5.
