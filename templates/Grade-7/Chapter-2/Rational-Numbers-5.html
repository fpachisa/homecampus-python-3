2.4.3 Multiplication and Division of Rational Numbers
To multiply two rational numbers:
  • First, convert any mixed numbers to improper fractions.
  • Next, multiply the numerators.
  • Then, multiply the denominators.
  • Lastly, simplify the resulting fraction if possible.
