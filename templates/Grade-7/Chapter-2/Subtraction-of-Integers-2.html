Example 14:
Evaluate each of the following:
a) 
+7 − 5

b) 
3 − 9

c) 
(−2) − 4
Solution 14:
a)
+7 − 5 = +7 + (−5)
This is the same as adding integers with different signs. Subtract 5 from 7 (7 − 5 = 2) and put the sign of the larger number (+) in front. The answer is +2 or simply 2.
