Rules of the number line...
  • Rule 1: Positive numbers appear to the right of the zero.
  • Rule 2: Negative numbers appear to the left of the zero.
  • Rule 3: Numbers increase in value going from left to right; so, for any given number - negative, positive or zero - all numbers to its left are smaller than it while all numbers to its right are greater than it.
