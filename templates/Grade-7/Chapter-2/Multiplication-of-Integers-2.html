Example 17:
Evaluate each of the following:
a) 6 × (−5)
b) (−20) × (−20)
c) (−61) × 0
d) (−4) × (−12) × (−2)
Solution 17:
a)
6 × (−5)
This is 6 times repeated addition of (−5).
(−5) + (−5) + (−5) + (−5) + (−5) + (−5) = −30
b)
(−20) × (−20) = − [20 × (−20)]
This is 20 times addition of (−20).
−[20 × (−20)] = − [−400] = 400
c) (−61) × 0 = 0
Note: The product of a number and zero is zero. Zero is neither positive nor negative.
d) (−4) × (−12) × (−2)
=  [(−)4 × (−)12] × (−2)
=  (+48) × (−2) = −96
