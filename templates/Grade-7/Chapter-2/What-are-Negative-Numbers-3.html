Example 2:
A company makes a negative annual profit of $50500. Express this figure using both positive and negative numbers.

Solution 2:
Annual profit of the company = −$50500
A minus sign is used to indicate loss (negative profit).
So, the above figure can also be expressed as a loss.
Annual loss of the company = $50500
