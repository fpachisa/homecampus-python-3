Solution 9:
The surface of the lake is considered to be at 0 m. Distances below the surface are considered to be negative.
Depth at which the fish is spotted at first = −6
Depth at which the fish is spotted later
= (−6) + (−5)
Start at negative 6 on the number line and move −5 units to the left. The answer is −11.
The fish is now at −11 m.
Alternative method:
When adding two integers with the same sign, First, add the absolute values (i.e. the values without the sign). In the example above, add 6 and 5 = 11.
