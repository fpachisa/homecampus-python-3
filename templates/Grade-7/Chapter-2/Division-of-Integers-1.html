2.3.2 Division
Divide the absolute values and follow the rules given below to determine the sign.
Rules of dividing integers...
  1. Dividing 2 integers with the same sign: The result is always positive.
    (+) ÷ (+) = (+)

  2. Dividing 2 integers with different signs: The result is always negative.
    (+) ÷ (−) = (−)
    (−) ÷ (+) = (−)
