2.2.4 Subtracting a negative integer
Example 15:
What is the value of (−6) − (−8)?
Solution 15:
Negative of x = (−x)
Negative of (−x) = −(− x) = +x
So, (−6) − (−8) = −6 + 8
This is the same as adding two integers with different signs.
Hence, (−6) − (−8) = −6 + 8 = 2
