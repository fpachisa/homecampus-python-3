2.1.3 Number Line
All numbers, whether positive, negative or zero, can be represented on the number line. A number line extends in both the directions as shown by arrows at the two ends.
Steps to draw a number line:
  • Draw a line (horizontally or vertically) and mark zero on it as a focus point.
  • Mark positive numbers +1, +2, +3 and so on to the right of zero such that they are evenly spaced.
  • Mark negative numbers −1, −2, −3 and so on to the left of zero at equal unit length.
  • Draw arrows at both the ends of the number line.
