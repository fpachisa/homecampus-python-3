2.2.3 Subtracting a positive integer
Example 13:
The temperature of a city is −4 °C. It further drops by 5 °C. What is the temperature of the city now?
Solution 13:
Method 1:
Temperature of the city at first = −4 °C
Temperature of the city after the drop
=  −4 − (5)
=  −4 − 5
=  −9 °C
Method 2:
Subtracting a number is the same as adding the opposite of the number. Opposite of + is − and vice versa. Here, the opposite of 5 or +5 is (−5).
So, −4 − 5 = −4 + (−5) and hence, the rules of addition of integers with the same sign can be applied here.
