2.4.2 Addition and Subtraction of Rational Numbers
The rules of operations on rational numbers are the same as those on integers we learnt in an earlier section. To add or subtract rational numbers, first convert any mixed numbers to improper fractions, then express all numbers as equivalent fractions with the same denominator.
