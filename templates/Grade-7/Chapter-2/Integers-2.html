Example 4:
On a certain day, Roy received a monthly salary of $7000 in his bank account. He withdrew $500 from it later on the same day. How would these transactions appear in the financial statement of his bank account?
Solution 4:
Money credited, received or deposited into a bank account is represented as a positive number in the financial statement.
Money debited, deducted or withdrawn from a bank account is represented as a negative number in the financial statement.
Money credited = +$7000
Money debited = −$500
Balance available = $6500
