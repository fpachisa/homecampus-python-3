Example 16:
Evaluate −3 − (+17).
Solution 16:
−3 − (+17) = −3 − 17
This is the same as adding two integers with the same sign.
Hence, −3 − (+17) = −3 − 17 = −20
Rules of subtracting integers...
  • Subtracting a positive integer: It is the same as adding a negative integer.
  • Subtracting a negative integer: It is the same as adding a positive integer.
