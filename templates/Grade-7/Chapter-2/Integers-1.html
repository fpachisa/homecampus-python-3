2.1.2 Integers
Integers are the set of numbers that include positive numbers (1, 2, 3, ...), zero and negative numbers (−1, −2, −3, ...), provided those numbers are non-fractional.
Example 3:
Which of the following are integers?
a) −45
b) +

1
__

2

c) +0.59
d) 21
e) −

100
___

9

f) 0
Solution 3:
Only −45, 21 and 0 are integers. The rest of the numbers are fractions and hence cannot be integers.
