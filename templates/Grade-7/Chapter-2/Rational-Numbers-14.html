Solution 31:
To multiply a decimal by another decimal:
  • First, ignore the decimal points and multiply the two decimals like you would multiply two whole numbers.
  • Then, count the number of decimal places (i.e. the number of digits after the decimal point) in each of the given numbers and add them up. This sum becomes the number of decimal place in the answer.
  • Lastly, place the decimal point in the answer counting the digits from right.
