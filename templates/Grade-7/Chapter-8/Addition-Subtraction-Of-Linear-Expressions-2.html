8.1 Addition and Subtraction of Linear Expressions

When we simplify an algebraic expression, we arrange the like terms together. Only like terms can be added or subtracted.
Example 1:
Given the following algebraic terms, group all the like terms together:

5m, −13x2y, 8mn, 2n3, 2xy, −5x, 3mn, 17y, 10xy2, −9m, −xy, 5yx2, −2xy
Solution 1:
Like termsTerms with no like variables
5m and −9m10xy2
−13x2y and 5yx22n3
8mn and 3mn−5x
