Factorise 16x + 24y
Solution 8:
Step 1:
The common factor in both the algebraic terms
(16x and 24y) is 8.

Step 2: 16x + 24y = 8(2x) + 8(3y)

Step 3: 16x + 24y = 8(2x + 3y)
