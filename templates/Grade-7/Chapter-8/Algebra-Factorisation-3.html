Solution 9:
a)
14mn − 21mp

Step 1: 7 and m are common factors.

Step 2: 14mn − 21mp = 7m(2n) − 7m(3p)

Step 3: 14mn − 21mp = 7m(2n − 3p)

**Note: 7m(2n − 3p) is incomplete factorisation because the common factor m has not been factored out.

b)
11a + 22a2 − 44ab
Step 1:
11 and a are factors common to all the three algebraic terms.
Step 2:
11a + 22a2 − 44ab = 11a(1) + 11a(2a) − 11a(4b)

Step 3: 11a + 22a2 − 44ab = 11a(1 + 2a − 4b)

** Note: Expand 11a(2a − 4b). We get 22a2 − 44ab. The algebraic term 11a is missing as given in the original algebraic expression.
