Example 4:
The masses of 3 sacks of wheat grains are (2u + 3v − 2)kg, (5u + 4v + 3)kg and
(u + 9v + 1)kg. If the price of wheat grains is $1.25 per kg, find the total price of the 3 sacks of wheat grains; given that u = ½ and v = ¼.
Solution 4:
Total mass of 3 sacks of wheat grains
= (2u + 3v - 2) + (5u + 4v + 3) + (u + 9v + 1)
= 8u + 16v + 2
= 8 × 1/2 + 16 × 1/4 +2
= 4 + 4 + 2
= 10 Kg
Total price of 3 sacks of wheat grains
= $1.25/kg × 10 Kg
= $12.50
