A composite number is a product of prime numbers.
Example 6:
Is 61 a prime or a composite number?

Solution 6:
To determine whether 61 is a prime, we divide it by the prime numbers 2, 3, 5, 7, ... less than 61.
We find that, 61 is only divisible by 1 and itself.
∴ 61 is a prime number.
