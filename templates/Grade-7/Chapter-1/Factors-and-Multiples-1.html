1.1 Factors and Multiples
1.1.1 Factors
Any whole number can be expressed as a product of two or more whole numbers. Those numbers then are called its factors.
Example 1:
Find the factors of 8.

Solution 1:
Here are the different ways we can multiply two or more whole numbers to get 8:
8 = 1 × 8
8 = 2 × 16
In other words...
8 is divisible by each of its factors.
1, 2, 4 and 8 are called factors of 8.
Interesting...
1 is a factor of every number. Every number is a factor of itself.
