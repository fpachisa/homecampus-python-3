Example 14:
The area of a square is 5625 cm2. What is the length of its side?

Solution 14:
Area = Length × Length
∴ Length = √Area 
5625
 = 
32 × 54

 = 
(3 × 52)2

√5625 
 = 
3 × 52

 = 
75
The length of its side is 75 cm.
