Example 4:
2 and 4 are factors of 8. Is 8 a multiple of both 2 and 4?
Solution 4:
Here are the multiples of 2.
2 × 1,
  2
2 × 2,
  4
2 × 3,
  6
2 × 4,
  8
2 × 5,
  10
...,
...
Here are the multiples of 4.
4 × 1,
  4
4 × 2,
  8
4 × 3,
  12
4 × 4,
  16
4 × 5,
  20
...,
...
From the above it is clear that 8 is a multiple of both 2 and 4.
