1.5 Lowest Common Multiple (LCM)
The lowest of the common multiples of two or more numbers is known as the lowest common multiple or least common multiple (LCM) of those numbers.
Example 11:
Find the LCM of 12 and 18.

Solution 11:
Method 1
Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84, 96, 108, 120, ...
Multiples of 18: 18, 36, 54, 72, 90, 108, 126, 144, 162, ...
Common multiples of 12 and 18: 36, 72, 108..
Lowest of the common multiples of 12 and 18: 36
Therefore, LCM = 36
