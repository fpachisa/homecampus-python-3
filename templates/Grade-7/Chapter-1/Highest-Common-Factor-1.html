1.4 Highest Common Factor (HCF)
The highest of the common factors of two or more numbers is known as the highest common factor (HCF) or greatest common factor (GCF) or greatest common divisor (GCD).
Example 9:
Find the HCF of 36 and 120.

Solution 9:
Method 1
Factors of 36: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18, 36
Factors of 120: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 12, 15, 20, 24, 30, 40, 60, 120
Common factors of 36 and 120:1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12
Highest of the common factors of 36 and 120: 12
∴ HCF = 12
