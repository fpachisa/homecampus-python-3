Example 2:
Find all the factors of 36.

Solution 2:
36 = 1 × 36
36 = 2 × 18
36 = 3 × 12
36 = 4 × 9
36 = 6 × 6
The factors of 36 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18 and 36.
Little tips...
To ensure that you do not skip any factors of 36,
you only need to check the numbers between
2 and the square root of 36 for its factors.
