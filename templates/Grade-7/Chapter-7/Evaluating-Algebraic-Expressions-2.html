Example 9:
Joan is left with $6.50 after buying 3 candies at x cents each. How much money did she have at first, if
a)   x = 5
b)   x = 15
Solution 9:
Total money spent on candies = 3x cents = $0.03x
Let p be the sum of money she had at first.
Then, p = $6.50 + $0.03x
a)
Substituting the value of x = 5, we get
p = $6.50 + $0.03 × 5 = $6.65
∴ She had $6.65 at first.
