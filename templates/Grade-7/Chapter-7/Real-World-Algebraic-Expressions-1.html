7.4 Translating Real-World Situations into Algebraic Expressions
Example 12:
The annual income of Fred is 10 times that of Glen.

a)
Find the annual income of Fred given that Glen's annual income is p.
b)
Let Fred's annual income be q. Express the relation between annual incomes of Fred and Glen using a formula.
Solution 12:
a)
Glen's annual income = p

Since, Fred's annual income is 10 times that of Glen,

∴ Fred's annual income = 10p

b)
Using (a), we get the formula, q = 10p
