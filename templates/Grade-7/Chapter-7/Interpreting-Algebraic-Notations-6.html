Example 6:
Write down an algebraic expression for each of the following statements:
a) Subtract 11z from the product of 3x and 9y.
b) Divide the difference of 8b and square of 3a by c.
c)
Subtract the square of the difference between b and c from the square of the difference between a and b.

d)
Subtract the quotient of 2p divided by 3q from the square of r.
e) r to the power of 4 divided by the sum of 5s and 4t.
Solution 6:
a)
Product of 3x and 9y = 3x × 9y = 27xy
Subtract 11z from 27xy = 27xy − 11z
Hence, the required expression is 27xy − 11z.

b)
Square of 3a = (3a)2 = 9a2
Difference of 8b and 9a2 = 8b − 9a2
