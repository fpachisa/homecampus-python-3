Example 10:
The average, A of 3 numbers a, b and c is given by the formula A = (a + b + c) ÷ 3. If a = 15, b = 20 and c = 13, find their average.
Solution 10: A = (a + b + c) ÷ 3
Substituting the variables by their values in the above formula, we get
A
= (15 + 20 + 13) ÷ 3

= 48 ÷ 3 = 16
∴ Their average is 16.
Example 11:
The circumference, C metres, of a circular field of radius, r metres, is given by the formula C = 2πr. Find the circumference of a circular field of radius 35 m.
Solution 11: C = 2πr
When r = 35,
C = 2π(35) = 70π
∴ The circumference of the circular field is 70π m.
