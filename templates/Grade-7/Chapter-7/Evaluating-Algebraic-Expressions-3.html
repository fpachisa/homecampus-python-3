7.3.2 Formulae

Formulae (the plural of formula) is the way of representing mathematical relationship between 2 or more variables.

We have learnt that the average of 3 numbers is given by

Average = (Sum of 3 numbers) ÷ 3

Let x, y and z be the 3 numbers, and let A be the average of 3 numbers, then their relationship can be expressed as:

A = (x + y + z) ÷ 3

The above relationship among the variables A, x, y and z is called a formula. To find the value of A, we substitute the values of x, y, and z in the formula.
