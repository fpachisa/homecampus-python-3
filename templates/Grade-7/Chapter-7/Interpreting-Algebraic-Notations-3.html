Example 5:
Jessie is selling burgers and hot dogs at a carnival. The price of a burger is $3 and the price of a hot dog is $1. Find the total price of
a) 2 burgers and 5 hot dogs
b) p burgers and 4 hot dogs
c) m burgers and n hot dogs
Solution 5:
a)
Price of 2 burgers
= $3 × 2

Price of 5 hot dogs
= $1 × 5

∴ Total price
= $(3 × 2 + 1 × 5)

= $11
