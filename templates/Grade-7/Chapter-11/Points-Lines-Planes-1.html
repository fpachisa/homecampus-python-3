11.1 Points, Lines and Planes
Geometry deals with points, lines, shapes and space. Let us study these basic terms in detail.
11.1.1 Points

P and Q are two points.

Characteristics of a point:
  1. It is represented by a dot (.) or a cross (x) and is usually named using a capital letter.

  2. It has no size.

  3. It has an exact position.

  4. 3 or more points lying in a straight line are called collinear.
