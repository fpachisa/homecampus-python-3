11.1.2 Lines

Characteristics of a line:
  1. A line, straight or curved, is a collection of infinite number of points.

  2. Has no width and no height.

  3. A straight line can be determined by two points. Example, Points P and Q above determine the straight line PQ or QP.

  4. The two arrowheads indicate that the line can be extended indefinitely in both directions.
