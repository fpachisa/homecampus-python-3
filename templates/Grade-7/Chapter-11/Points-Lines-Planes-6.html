The surface of a basketball is not flat. Hence, it is called a curved surface.

Characteristics of a Plane:
  1. A plane extends indefinitely in two dimensions: length and width. It has no height or thickness.
  2. It is a flat surface.
  3. Any three non-collinear points lie on one plane. 3 points name a plane. Example, In the figure below, Points A, B and C form Plane ABC.
