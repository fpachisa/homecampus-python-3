11.1.4 Rays

Characteristics of a ray:
  1. It extends indefinitely in one direction (as shown by the arrowhead) but ends at a single point in the other direction, called the end point of the ray.

  2. When naming a ray, the end point is named first. It is written as AB where A is the end point.
