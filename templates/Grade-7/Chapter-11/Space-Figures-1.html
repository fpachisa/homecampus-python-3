11.1.6 Space

Cylinders (cans), spheres (balls), square and triangular prisms are all 3 dimensional geometric figures or space figures.

Characteristics of a Space:
  1. Space is 3 dimensional: possesses length, width and height.

  2. It extends indefinitely in all directions.

  3. It consists of planes, lines and points.
