11.1.3 Line Segments

Characteristics of a line segment:
  1. Two points, E and F, when joined together form a straight line segment, written as EF or FE. A line segment is a part of a straight line between two end points.

  2. A line segment has a finite length determined by the two end points as opposed to a line which has an infinite length.
