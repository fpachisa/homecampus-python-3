4.3 Proportion

A proportion means that two ratios are equal.

It can be written as two equal fractions

p
__

q

=

r
__

s

or

by using colons p : q = r : s

It is read as 'p is to q as r is to s'.

Let us consider the ratios 26 : 13 and 2 : 1.

We cross multiply to check if the two ratios are equal and form a proportion.

26 × 1 = 26

13 × 2 = 26.

Hence, the ratios 26 : 13 and 2 : 1, are proportional to each other.
