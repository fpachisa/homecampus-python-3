Solution 13:
Let the salaries of Nina and Tina be $5x and $7x.
7x − 5x = 560
2x = 560 ⇛ x = 280
Hence, monthly salary of Nina = 5 × $280 = $1400
And, monthly salary of Tina = 7 × $280 = $1960
