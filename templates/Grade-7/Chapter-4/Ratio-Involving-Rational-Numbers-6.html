Example 7:
The ratio of two numbers is 5 : 6. If their sum is equal to 33, find their difference.
Solution 7:
Let the two numbers be 5x and 6x.
Sum = 11x
11x = 33
Hence, x = 3
Difference = 6x − 5x = x = 3
