Example 21:
A mason is making a brick wall. It takes him 5 minutes to lay 3 bricks on the wall. How many bricks will he have laid on the wall after one hour?

Solution 21:

Number of bricks laid : Time taken = 3 : 5

Let x be the number of bricks that are laid in 1 hour i.e. 60 minutes.

3 : 5 = x : 60

x = 3 ×

60
___

5

x = 36

He will have laid 36 bricks in 1 hour.
