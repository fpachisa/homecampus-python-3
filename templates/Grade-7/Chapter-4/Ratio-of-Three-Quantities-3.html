Solution 10: (contd.)
Convert each ratio to an equivalent ratio where the value of b in the final ratio is the LCM of the original values of b.

LCM of 4 and 5 is 20. Hence, the value of b in the ratio a : b : c is 20.

a : b = 3 × 5 : 4 × 5 = 15 : 20

b : c = 5 × 4 : 6 × 4 = 20 : 24

a : b : c = 15 : 20 : 24
