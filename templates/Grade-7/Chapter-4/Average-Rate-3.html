Example 17:
On a certain day, 400.20 USD could be exchanged for 500 SGD.

a)
Find the exchange rate between the two currencies, correct to 4 decimal places,
i) From USD to SGD
ii) From SGD to USD

b)
If you exchanged 230 SGD to USD, how much money would you receive in USD?

c)
Mrs. Yang's son, studying in New York, needed 12000 USD. How much amount, in SGD, did she transfer?

Solution 17:
a) 500 SGD = 400.20 USD
i) 1 SGD
= 400.20 ÷ 500 USD

= 0.8004 USD
The exchange rate is 0.8004 USD to 1 SGD.
ii) 1 USD
= 500 ÷ 400.20 SGD

= 1.2493 SGD
The exchange rate is 1.2493 SGD to 1 USD.
