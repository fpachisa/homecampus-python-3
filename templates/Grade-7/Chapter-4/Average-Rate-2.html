In other words...

Rate is a value that describes one quantity (e.g. runs) in terms of another quantity (e.g. over).

Example 15:
A construction worker receives $100 for working 8 hours a day. What is his hourly wage rate?

Solution 15:
Wage rate
= $100 ÷ 8 hours

= $12.50 per hour

Example 16:
A train covers a distance of 500 km in 4 hours. What is the rate at which the train moves in km/h?

Solution 16:
Rate
= 500 km ÷ 4 hours

= 125 km/h

Note that this rate is the average speed of the train.
