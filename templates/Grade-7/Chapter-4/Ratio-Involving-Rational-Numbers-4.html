Solution 4:
a) Ratio of typing speed of Sam to that of Frank
= 52 : 76
= 52 ÷ 4 : 76 ÷ 4 (HCF of 52 and 76 is 4)
= 13 : 19
b) Ratio of typing speed of Frank to that of Sam
= 76 : 52
= 19 : 13
c) 13 : 19 ≠ 19 :13
∴ The two ratios are not equal.
As a rule,
  • A ratio has no unit, and
  • Generally, a:b ≠ b:a
