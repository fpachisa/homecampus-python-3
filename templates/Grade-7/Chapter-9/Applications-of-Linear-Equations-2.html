Example 5:
The sum of 5 consecutive integers is 160. Find the largest of the 5 integers.

Solution 5:
Let x be the smallest integer.

Second consecutive integer = x + 1

Third consecutive integer = x + 2

Fourth consecutive integer = x + 3

Fifth consecutive integer = x + 4

Sum of the 5 consecutive integers = 160

x + (x + 1) + (x + 2) + (x + 3) + (x + 4) = 160

5x + 10 = 160

5x = 150

x = 30

∴ The largest integer = x + 4

= 30 + 4

= 34
