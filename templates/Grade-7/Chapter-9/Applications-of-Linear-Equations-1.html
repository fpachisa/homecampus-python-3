9.3 Applications of Linear Equations
Linear equations find uses in real life situations. We must learn how to formulate them from the available data.
Example 4:
Lilac will be 7 years old 3 years from now. Find her present age.

Solution 4:
Let the present age of Lilac be x years.

Age after 3 years = (x + 3) years

Given,

x + 3 = 7

Solving the linear equation,

x = 7 − 3

= 4

∴ Lilac's present age is 4 years.
