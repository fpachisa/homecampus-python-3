Example 2:
Solve the following linear equations:

a)
2x − 5 = x + 4
b)
−3x − 3 = −5x − 5
c)
2(2x + 4) = 3x + 12
Solution 2:
a)
2x − 5 = x + 4
Subtract x from both sides.
2x − 5 − x
=  x + 4 − x

x − 5
=  4
Add 5 to both sides.
x − 5 + 5
=  4 + 5

∴ x
=  9
Cross check:
Substitute x with 9 in the original equation.
LHS: 2x − 5 = 2 × 9 − 5 = 13
RHS: x + 4 = 9 + 4 = 13
Hence, LHS = RHS
