−3x − 3 + 5x

2x − 3
=  −5

=  −5 + 3

2x

=  −1
c)
2(2x + 4) = 3x + 12
Apply distributive law.
4x + 8
=  3x + 12
Subtract 3x from both sides.
4x + 8 − 3x
=  3x + 12 − 3x

x + 8
=  12

x + 8 − 8
=  12 − 8

∴ x
=  4
