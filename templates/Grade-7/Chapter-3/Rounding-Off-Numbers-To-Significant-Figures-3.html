Rule 3: In a decimal, all zeros before a non-zero digit are not significant. They are used as placeholders i.e. they keep other figures in their correct places.
Example 7:
State the number of significant figures in each number:
a) 0.35   b) 0.07   c) 0.00081962

Solution 7:
a) The number 0.35 has 2 significant figures.
b) The number 0.07 has 1 significant figure.
c) The number 0.00081962 has 5 significant figures.
