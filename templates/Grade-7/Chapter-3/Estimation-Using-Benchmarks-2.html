Example 14:
A man observes that he covers a distance of 0.87 m in one step. If he takes 9 steps to cover the length of a room and 7 steps to cover the breadth of the room, find the approximate perimeter of the rectangular room.

Solution 14:
Distance covered in one step can be used as a benchmark to find the dimensions of the rectangular room.
Length and breadth of the room measured in steps = 7 + 9 = 16 steps.
Distance covered in 16 steps
= 16 × 0.87 m

≈ 16 × 1 m

= 16 m
Perimeter of the rectangular room
= 2 × 16 m = 32 m
