Example 3:
Round off 89.451 to the nearest:
a) tenth   b) hundredth

Solution 3:
a) Rounding off a number to the nearest tenth means rounding off to 1 decimal place.
89.451
Rounding digit = 4
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 5
Since 5 ≥ 5, add 1 to the rounding digit.
∴ 89.451 is rounded up to 89.5.
b) Rounding off a number to the nearest hundredth means rounding off to 2 decimal places.
89.451
Rounding digit = 5
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 1
Since 1 < 5, the rounding digit is unchanged.
∴ 89.451 is rounded down to 89.45.
