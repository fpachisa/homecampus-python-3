Rule 4: In a decimal, all zeros after a non-zero digit are significant.
Example 8:
State the number of significant figures in each number:
a) 0.250   b) 7.070   c) 1.200

Solution 8:
a) The number 0.250 has 3 significant figures. The zero before 2 is not significant. It is used as a placeholder.
b) The number 7.070 has 4 significant figures.
c) The number 1.200 has 4 significant figures.
Rule 5: The trailing zeroes in a whole number are not significant. They are used as placeholders.
