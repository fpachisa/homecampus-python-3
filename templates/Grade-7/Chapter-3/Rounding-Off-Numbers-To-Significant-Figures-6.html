Solution 10:
a) 75 491
7 is the first significant figure.
Rounding digit = 7
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 5
Since 5 ≥ 5, add 1 to the rounding digit.
Hence, 75 491 = 80 000, correct to 1 significant figure.
Here, put 4 zeros as placeholders. As per Rule 5 above, these zeros are not significant.
b) 75 491
5 is the second significant figure.
Rounding digit = 5
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 4
Since 4 < 5, the rounding digit is unchanged.
Hence, 75 491 = 75 000, correct to 2 significant figures.
