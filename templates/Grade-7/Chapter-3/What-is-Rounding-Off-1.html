3.1 What is Rounding Off?
"According to the US Bureau of Transit Statistics, there were 243,023,485 registered passenger vehicles in the US in 2004. Out of these roughly 243 million vehicles, 136 million were classified as cars, while 92 million were classified as "Other 2 axle, 4 tire vehicles" like SUVs and pick-up trucks. Yet another 6 million were classified as vehicles with 2 axles and 6 tires and 2 million were classified as "Truck, combination". There were approximately 6 million motorcycles in the US in 2004."
The above extract uses approximate figures instead of exact or actual figures for each type of vehicle. This is because the approximate values are easier to work with and remember. When we simplify a number to determine an approximate value, it is called rounding off or approximation.
