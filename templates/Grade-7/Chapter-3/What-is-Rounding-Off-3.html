b) 7 211 239 210
Rounding digit is digit in the ten thousands place which is 3.
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 9
Since 9 ≥ 5, add 1 to the rounding digit.
∴ The population is 7 211 240 000, correct to the nearest 10000.

c) 7 211 239 210
Rounding digit is digit in the millions place which is 1.
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 2
Since 2 < 5, the rounding digit is unchanged.
∴ The population is 7 211 000 000, correct to the nearest million.
