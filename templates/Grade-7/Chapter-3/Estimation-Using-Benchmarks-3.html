Example 15:
A vanity box has 6 identical divisions. Each division is 9.8 cm long and 7.2 cm wide. Estimate the
a) dimensions of each division
b) area of the box
Solution 15:
Dimensions of one division can be used as a benchmark to find the area of the box.
a) Dimensions of each division:
Length = 9.8 cm ≈ 10 cm
Breadth = 7.2 cm ≈ 7 cm
b) Area of the box:
Total length ≈ 3 × 10 cm = 30 cm
Total breadth ≈ 2 × 7 cm = 14 cm
Area of the box ≈ 30 × 14 = 420 cm2
