Example 13:
Zoya purchased 4 packets of rice of different brands. Their prices were $17, $20, $22 and $21. How much did she pay for them? Give an estimated price.

Solution 13:
Since the numbers are close to each other, a cluster estimate can be used to estimate the sum.
The price of each brand of rice is about $20. Thus, we can take $20 as the cluster value.
The total estimated price of the 4 packets of rice = $20 × 4 = $80
