3.1.2 Rounding off Numbers to Decimal Places
Rounding off a decimal number is similar to rounding off a whole number. We use the following rules.
Rules of rounding off to desired number of decimal places...
  • Round up: If the digit to the right of rounding digit is 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9, add one to the rounding digit and drop all the digits to the right of it.
  • Round down: If the digit to the right of rounding digit is 0, 1, 2, 3 or 4, keep the rounding digit unchanged and drop all the digits to the right of it.
