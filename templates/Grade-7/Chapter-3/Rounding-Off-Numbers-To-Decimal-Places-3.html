Example 4:
A student observes the length of a rare species of lizard to be 0.95205 m. Round off the length to:
a) 1 decimal place   b) 4 decimal places

Solution 4:
a) Length of the lizard = 0.95205 m
Rounding digit = 9
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 5
Since 5 ≥ 5, add 1 to the rounding digit.
∴ The length of the lizard = 1.0 m
b) Length of the lizard = 0.95205 m
Rounding digit = 0
Digit to the right of rounding digit = 5
Since 5 ≥ 5, add 1 to the rounding digit.
∴ The length of the lizard = 0.9521 m
